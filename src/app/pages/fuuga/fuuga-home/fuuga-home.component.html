Welcome to FUUGA page

FUUGA means federated ugwueme undergraduate Association.
Fuuga was formed in the year 1999 but was inaugurated in the year 2000. It was formed with the believe that increase in the number of graduates in ugwueme will bring about a corresponding increase in human and material development in Ugwueme. It was also believed that Education is the surest way to bring about development, unity and wiping away of the growing discrimination among the two autonomous community in Ugwueme.
